Rainbow

The colorful arc of a rainbow, as may appear after rain. Generally depicted as the left half of a full rainbow, showing six bands of color: red, orange, yellow, green, blue, and violet 🌈

The colours of the rainbow are known as "ROYGBIV" OR "VIBGYOR" 'R' stands for red, 'O' stands for orange, 'Y' stands for yellow, 'G' stands for green, 'B' stands for blue, 'I' stands for indigo and 'V' stands for violet.
